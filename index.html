The 2020 AFL Season

The 2020 AFL season, though interrupted by the COVID outbreak, was successfully played out. Port Adelaide took home the minor premiership after the shortened 17 home and away games, with no games being played in Victoria after Week 4. Still, it was two Victorian sides who played the final at the GABBA, won by Richmond over Geelong.
