The 2020 AFL Season

The 2020 AFL season, though interrupted by the COVID outbreak, was successfully played out. Port Adelaide took home the minor premiership after the shortened 17 home and away games, with no games being played in Victoria after Week 4. Still, it was two Victorian sides who played the final at the GABBA, won by Richmond over Geelong.

Highs and Lows in Adelaide

Port Adelaide secured the minor premiership in 2020 with 14 wins over Brisbane on percentage. Adelaide, the other team in the city, won the wooden spoon, finishing last with 3 wins below North Melbourne also on percentage. The eventual premiers, Richmond, secured a third position, whilst their Grand Final opponent Geelong secured fourth. It was the first time in decades that neither of the top 2 teams made the Grand Final
